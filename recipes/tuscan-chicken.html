Tuscan Chicken

This is one of my family's staple dishes to make, and it's so delicious and satisfying. I've tweaked this recipe over the years and it's pretty much perfect.

Ingredients

  • Angel hair pasta (enough for a few servings)
  • 2-3 chicken breasts
  • Baby spinach (about a handful)
  • 1/2 pint of heavy cream
  • Splash of chicken broth (optional)
  • Half of a yellow onion, finely diced
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic
  • Salt, pepper, and oregano
  • Parmesan
  • 1 jar of Bella Sun Luci Sun Dried Tomato Bruschetta (or a similar product)

Steps

  1. Bring a lightly-oiled skillet or pan to medium high heat. Rinse, trim, and season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and oregano to taste. Cook for 4 minutes on each side, then remove to rest.
  2. In a bigger, lightly-oiled saucepan, add the onion and garlic with a dash of oregano and cook on medium-low until the onion is translucent.
  3. While the onions are cooking, bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the angel hair and cook for 4 minutes.
  4. Add a splash of chicken broth and the jar of bruschetta to the pan, and stir for a minute. Then, add the heavy cream and stir until the sauce becomes a uniform color.
  5. Drain the pasta. Add the spinach to the sauce and stir for a minute until the spinach is wilted. Cut up the chicken and add to the sauce, and stir until everything is well-mixed.
  6. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and serve with the pasta.
